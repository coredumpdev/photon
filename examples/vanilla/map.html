Photon — Web Mercator vector-tile basemap, zero libraries

Everything is Photon core: MVT decode, ear-clipping triangulation, tile math and WebGL2 rendering — no Mapbox / MapLibre / Leaflet. Tiles here are MapLibre's keyless demo set (world countries). Point it at any OpenMapTiles .pbf endpoint (MapTiler, a self-hosted tileserver) for full street detail. Pink dots are data overlaid via lonLatToWorld. Drag to pan, wheel to zoom, click a region to read its feature properties, click a dot for its label.
